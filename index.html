Who We Are

Shriniwasa Roadways Private Limited (SRPL) forms the beating heart of the Shriniwasa Group, delivering comprehensive end‑to‑end logistics solutions throughout India. With an annual turnover exceeding ₹550 crores and decades of experience, SRPL has pioneered containerised cargo movement and multi‑modal logistics services. Our journey began in 1974 as a humble road transporter handling thousands of tonnes of scrap and paper each month, and we formalised the group in 1987 when SRPL laid the foundation of today’s multi‑crore enterprise. From those early days we have steadily evolved into a trusted partner for enterprises across manufacturing, chemicals, FMCG, steel and more.

Why Choose Shriniwasa

Optimised Operations

We treat your cargo like our own. Our supply chain management is optimised to provide real‑time tracking and monitoring with leak‑proof packaging, anti‑pilferage devices and just‑in‑time deliveries. Advanced SAP‑based operations ensure transparency and no‑gap reporting.

Robust Infrastructure

Our dedicated fleet includes specialised trucks, trailers, cranes and forklifts alongside more than 430 ISO containers and additional contracted vehicles. We operate 42 warehouses across 27 locations with over 262,000 sq ft of containerised warehousing space to keep your goods secure.

National Reach

A vast network of offices and warehouses allows us to serve every corner of the country. From major metros to remote locations, our multimodal capability—road, rail and sea—means your cargo arrives exactly when you need it. Over four decades of innovation ensure we remain ahead of the curve.
