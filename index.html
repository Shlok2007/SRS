Our Story

Founded in 1974 by visionary entrepreneur P. C. Jain, Shriniwasa Roadways began its journey in road transportation with just one truck and a dream. Over the decades we have evolved into a pioneer of multimodal logistics—seamlessly integrating road, rail and sea movements—while staying rooted in our core values of trust, integrity and customer service. Today the Shriniwasa Group commands an annual turnover exceeding ₹550 crore and continues to expand its reach across the length and breadth of India.

Road Transport

Dedicated fleet of modern trucks and trailers, offering door‑to‑door connectivity across India with a focus on safety and punctuality.

Rail Logistics

Strategic partnerships with CONCOR and Indian Railways enable efficient containerised movement of cargo across the nation's rail network.

Sea Freight

Domestic coastal shipping solutions along India's western coast link major ports, reducing transit times and carbon footprint.
