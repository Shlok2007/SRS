Company Profile

Shriniwasa Roadways Private Limited (SRPL) is the flagship company of the Shriniwasa Group and one of India’s pioneers in multimodal logistics. Starting with a single truck in 1974, the company has grown into a nationwide network delivering containerised cargo through road, rail and sea. Our ISO 9001 certified processes, state‑of‑the‑art fleet and experienced professionals allow us to handle bulk transportation, special cargo and time‑critical consignments with unmatched reliability.

The foundation stone of the group was laid by Mr. P. C. Jain, a Masters graduate in Economics with a passion for business and social upliftment. Under his leadership and the guidance of our directors, Shriniwasa Roadways has achieved an annual turnover of over ₹550 crore and continues to expand its presence across India. We remain committed to providing end‑to‑end logistics solutions that drive our customers’ growth and improve lives.

Quality Policy

We are committed to providing proficient and cost‑effective multimodal logistics solutions to the trade and industry through a motivated team of professionals. Our aim is to achieve absolute customer satisfaction by continuously improving service levels while ensuring total compliance with our quality management system.

Our Focus

  • To provide total economical logistics solutions and end the age‑old costly road movement.
  • To deliver Just‑in‑Time services and cost reduction through integrated multimodal transport.
  • To offer professional warehousing and state‑of‑the‑art distribution exclusively for our customers.
  • To deploy experienced professionals and well‑trained labourers for warehousing and distribution.
  • To terminate hassles of monitoring warehouse stocks and inventories.
  • To reduce freight cost by using multiple modes of transportation.
  • To increase market share with innovative logistic solutions and dedicate owned fleet at each warehouse for fool‑proof distribution.
