Accreditations & Certifications

Our licences and certifications reflect our commitment to quality, safety, and compliance. These accreditations ensure we meet or exceed industry standards and reassure our clients that their cargo is in capable hands.

  • Authorised sole franchisee for CONCOR Salem – delivering rail logistics in partnership with the Container Corporation of India.
  • Operations and administration agreement with CONCOR Depot enabling efficient container handling and loading.
  • ISO 9001:2015 certified Quality Management System.
  • Registered business associates and authorised freight forwarders with leading ports and shipping lines.
  • IBA approval (Reg. No: CIS-1431) for nationwide trucking and logistics services.

Awards & Recognitions

Our dedication to delivering value to customers and continually improving our processes has earned us several accolades over the years.

  • Highest loaders of domestic containers by rail throughout India via CONCOR for the past seven years.
  • Highest loaders of railway wagons under the freight forwarding scheme for three consecutive years.
  • Best performance award for handling 60,000 MT of loose pig iron per annum from the Dempo Group of Companies.
  • Performance award for shunting 270,000 MT of coke per annum for India Cements Ltd.
  • Multiple COCOR-EXIM Star Awards recognising our outstanding contributions to rail logistics.
