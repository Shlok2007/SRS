Innovative Multimodal Logistics

At Shriniwasa Roadways we constantly push the boundaries of what’s possible in logistics. By combining road, rail and sea movements we offer flexible, cost‑effective and environmentally friendly transport options for our customers.

Road Movement

Our traditional road transport involves deploying a truck at your factory or warehouse. Once stuffed, it travels directly to your customer or distribution centre. The minimum requirement is a single truck load.

Rail Movement

Rail is a safer mode that requires a slightly higher quantity. We collect cargo from multiple locations, load it into containers and despatch a train with at least 70 containers. Through partnerships we can even accommodate single truck or container loads, catering to varied requirements.

Sea Movement

Domestic coastal shipping along India’s western coast is a new frontier. We collect cargo, move it to Cochin port and load it onto container vessels bound for Mumbai, Mundra or Kandla. Collaborations with shipping lines allow us to handle single container loads of 25–28 MT.
